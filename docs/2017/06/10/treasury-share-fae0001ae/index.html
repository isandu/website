treasury share fae0001ae

Jun 10, 2017

Remember:

Treasury shares are shares that a company buys back from the market and records them as a decrease in equity.

Outstanding shares = issued shares - treasury shares

Question

Which from below is not correct:

  1. Companies acquire treasury shares to avoid a takeover by an outside party
  2. Companies acquire treasury shares to increase the earnings per share
  3. Companies acquire treasury shares for employee share option compensation
  4. Companies acquire treasury shares to increase dividends to itself

Solution

Point 4.
