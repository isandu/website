Remember: The cost of any asset is the sum of all the costs incurred to bring the asset to its intended use.

Question: Company A paid $9,008 cash for equipment used in the business. At the time of purchase the equipment had a list price of $19,165. When the balance sheet was prepared, the market value of the equipment was $21,310. Which of the three amounts is the most relevant amount that should be used for measurement subsequent to initial recognition under the cost model?

  1. $9,008

  2. $19,165

  3. $21,310

  4. $9,008 at the day of the purchase, $21,310 at the balance sheet date

Solution: $21,310
