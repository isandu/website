Question

Which of the following shows a correct recording of the purchase of property, plant and equipment (net amount) in 2016?

  1. Increase the Property, plant and equipment account and the Accounts Payable account by €100

  2. Increase the Property, plant and equipment account and the Accounts Payable account by €143

  3. Increase the Property, plant and equipment account and the Accounts Payable account by €231

  4. Increase the Property, plant and equipment account and the Accounts Payable account by €65

Solution:

Beginning balance 1,900 + Purchases - Sales (Cash from sale of ppe 50 - Gain on sale of ppe 7 = sold ppe 43) - Depreciation, impairment 88 = Ending balance 2,000

Purchases = 2,000 + 88 + 43 - 1,900 = 231
