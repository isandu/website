Remember: Beginning inventory + Purchases - Cost of Goods Sold = Ending inventory

Beginning inventory of 2015 = Ending inventory of 2014

Question: The ending inventory for the year ended December 31, 2014, is understated. How will this erros affect the net income for 2014 and 2015?

  1. 2014 overstated; 2015 understated

  2. 2014 overstated; 2015 no effect

  3. 2014 overstated; 2015 no effect

  4. 2014 understated; 2015 overstated

Solution: Point 4.
