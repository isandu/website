Remember:

“Big bath” accounting refers to the downward manipulation of earnings by the managers.

Question

“Big bath” accounting refers to the situation when:

  1. Managers manipulate their earnings downwards by recording higher provisions than necessary
  2. Managers manipulate earnings upwards by selling treasury shares at a profit
  3. Managers manipulate earnings upwards by selling inventory at a too high discount
  4. Managers manipulate earnings upwards by disclosing lower contingent liabilities

Solution:

Point 1.
