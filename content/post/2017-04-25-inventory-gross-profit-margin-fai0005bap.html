Remember: Beginning balance + Increases - Decreases = Ending balance

Gross margin = (Sales - Cost of goods sold) / Sales

For Company Glamour it is known that the beginning inventory in January 2016 is $106,258, the net purchases of inventory during January 2016 are $619,329, the net sales revenue during January 2016 is $499,198 and the gross profit rate for January 2016 is 0.3 percent.

Question:

What is the company’s estimated cost of goods sold for the month of January?

Solution: $349,438.6

Question:

What is the company’s estimated ending inventory for the month of January?

Solution: $376,148.4
