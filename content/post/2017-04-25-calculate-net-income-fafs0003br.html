Remember: Beginning balance equity + Net income - Dividends +/- changes in other comprehensive income = Ending balance equity

Question: A transportation company balance sheet shows at December 31, 2016 and December 31, 2015 the following (in € million):

Account 2016 2015
Total assets 244 226
Total liabilities 76 40

During the year ended December 31, 2016, the company paid dividends of €118 million. There are no other changes in equity or other comprehensive income. What is the net income during the year ended December 31, 2016 (in € million)?

Solution: $100
