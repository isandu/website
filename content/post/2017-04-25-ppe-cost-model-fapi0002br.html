Remember: The cost of any asset is the sum of all the costs incurred to bring the asset to its intended use.

Question: Company A paid $10,788 cash for equipment used in the business. At the time of purchase the equipment had a list price of $19,315. When the balance sheet was prepared, the market value of the equipment was $20,886. Which of the three amounts is the most relevant amount that should be used for measurement subsequent to initial recognition under the cost model?

  1. $10,788

  2. $19,315

  3. $20,886

  4. $10,788 at the day of the purchase, $20,886 at the balance sheet date

Solution: Point 1.
