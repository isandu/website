AstraZeneca Plc is a British-Swedish multinational pharmaceutical company headquartered in London, United Kingdom. AstraZeneca prepares its consolidated financial statements in accordance with IFRS. AstraZeneca, alongside other pharmaceutical companies, is dependent on the success of its Research & Development (R&D) program. The R&D expenses include various costs like fees paid to doctors and participants in clinical trials, the cost of land and buildings used for research or development efforts, legal expenses for protecting patents, hardware to store clinical data.

Question: Assume that R&D expenditures are paid in cash. Use the financial statements of AstraZeneca Plc to record the journal entry made by AstraZeneca to recognize the R&D expenditures.

Solution: Decrease cash with $5,997 and increase Research and Development expenses with $5,997.
