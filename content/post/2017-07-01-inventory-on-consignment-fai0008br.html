Question Which of the following statements is correct:

  1. A retailer that holds inventory on consignment usually pays for inventory when the inventory is sold to customers

  2. A retailer that holds inventory on consignment usually records the purchase of inventory when the inventory is received

  3. A retailer that holds inventory on consignment usually records an inventory impairment if during the stock taking he realizes that the inventory is damaged

  4. A retailer that holds inventory on consignment usually pays for inventory when the inventory is received

Solution:

Point 1.
